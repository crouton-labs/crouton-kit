Input: $ARGUMENTS

Please prove or disprove this finding. I trust you, but I want additional opinions, so please spawn 2-3 advisor agents. 
Provide each with relevant files and context, but do NOT bias them with an outcome. Agents are suggestible, so asking them to prove something is true or false usually results in the agent just agreeing. Instead, ask them to try to validate or invalidate the hypothesis by providing concrete facts and supporting code snippets with line numbers to prove or disprove. They MUST not use words like "likely" or "probably"—if so, it's a sign they need to do more investigation.
